Assure provide an 'Employee Assistance Programme' [EAP] that has been designed to provide positive support to each of us.

The programme  has been implemented to support wellness within the studio. As discussed at the introduction to Assures' services, access to this programme is up to the individual - no referral is needed. The consultations you may choose to have are confidential and are at no cost to you when it falls within our EAP agreement. Details of their contracted services can be found in this programme outline. If services beyond what we are contracted for are required/suggested these will be outlined with the individual and the adoption of them are at their discretion.

Assure has clinical psychologists' able to assist 24/7 and they can be reached by calling 1800 505 015. This number has been added to the BVN phone list.

Assures' [EAP] recognises that any number of factors can effect your happiness and satisfaction in the workplace and the service has been designed to address big and small problems that arise either at work or in your personal life in a positive way.

The types of support and coaching that Assure may assist you [or your family] with include, but are not limited to:

  • General feelings of anxiety, stress or depression
  • Feeling overwhelmed by pressures placed on you in the workplace and/or situations at home
  • The difficulties families face with with work life balance
  • Personal issues with your partner or other people close to you
  • Family challenges such as divorce, serious illness or a death in the family
  • Conflict with a team member or members
  • Frustration with a lack of career progression
  • The struggle to successfully return to work after a period of extended leave due to injury, maternity leave, etc.

Generally, the EAP on offer will positively support anything effecting your mental health. Please feel able to access their services confidentially and with confidence.

Assure also provide online support and resources. There are some handy hints and tips here which may come in handy at work or home.  Instructions for how to access this portal can be found here.

We hope that this service will be useful to you. The studio supports wellbeing and hopes that you will benefit from this relationship with Assure.

If you have any further questions don't hesitate to speak with any of the Principals, Bianca or Mel.
