Styleguide

So that all our project sheets look the same and read as if they are written with one voice; here are some guidelines.

General

As a base we use the Economist style guide. Variations from that are detailed below.

Measurements

All measurements are to be in SI units or SI derivatives.

Areas are in m² or ha

Never use M², M2, m2, sqm, sq.m, Hectares etc.

Where a project sheet requires measurements in non-SI units (e.g. ft² when presenting project sheets to North American clients) include the measurements both in SI and non-SI units.

Words

Masterplan is one word (NOT master plan or master-plan)
