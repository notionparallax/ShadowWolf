Photos

We need photos of you all the time! Classy B&W photos for submissions, classy colour photos of you for other submissions, and fun photos for people's leaving books and that kind of thing.

To put in a new photo just get the url of the image and paste it into the field below the current image. We don't host images, so you need to get the url from open asset or from Tropos. You can even get images from outside so if there's a good image on Facebook then you can use that too.
